The Luxivo Story

Where timeless design meets modern conscience — a new era of conscious luxury.

Founded in the spirit of artistry

Luxivo was born from a desire to reimagine luxury — not as excess, but as intention. In 2018, our founder Elena Voss set out to create pieces that honor heritage craftsmanship while embracing contemporary silhouettes. Each Luxivo piece is a quiet statement: refined, enduring, and deeply personal.

We believe that true elegance is found in the details. From ethically sourced materials to partnerships with family-owned ateliers in Italy and France, every stitch tells a story. Our name, "Luxivo," blends luminosity and life — a philosophy that guides everything we create.

Today, Luxivo is a global destination for those who seek beauty without compromise: sustainable, inclusive, and endlessly inspiring.
