1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At Luxivo ("we," "us," "our"), privacy is not an afterthought—it is the foundation of our relationship with you. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website www.luxivo.com (the "Site"), make a purchase, or interact with our services.

✨ Luxivo Promise: We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is used solely to elevate your shopping experience.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Identifiers Name, email address, shipping/billing address, phone number.
  • Payment Details Credit card information (processed via secure PCI-DSS compliant gateways; we do not store full card details).
  • Account Credentials Username, password, and preferences when you create a Luxivo account.
  • Transaction History Products purchased, return information, and wishlist items.
  • Technical Data IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and browsing behavior via analytics.
  • Communications Records of your correspondence with our concierge team.

We collect this data directly from you (e.g., when you register, place an order, or subscribe to our newsletter) and automatically through cookies and similar technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

Luxivo uses your personal data for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Processing and fulfilling orders, including shipping and payment confirmation.
  • Communicating order updates, delivery status, and customer support inquiries.
  • Personalizing your shopping experience and recommending products based on preferences.
  • Sending marketing communications (only with your consent, which you may withdraw at any time).
  • Improving our Site, troubleshooting, and preventing fraud.
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms & Conditions.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share information with trusted third parties that help us operate our business:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), shipping carriers (DHL, FedEx), IT hosting, and email platforms.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred with notice.

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Luxivo uses cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance functionality, analyze trends, and remember your preferences. You can manage cookie settings via your browser, but disabling essential cookies may affect site features.

We use both session cookies (expire after closing your browser) and persistent cookies (remain until deleted). Third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) help us understand user behavior in an anonymized manner.

🍪 Your Choice: By using our Site, you consent to cookie placement. You may adjust your browser to refuse cookies, but some interactive features may be limited.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, firewalls, and restricted access to personal data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to protect your account credentials and notify us immediately of any suspected breach.

Luxivo is PCI DSS compliant, ensuring that payment data is handled with the highest level of security.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (including GDPR for EU residents and CCPA for California residents), you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your data, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails via the "unsubscribe" link or contact us.
  • Data Portability: Receive a machine-readable copy of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please email privacy@luxivo.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children’s Privacy

Luxivo does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us, and we will promptly delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Luxivo is based in the United States, and your data may be processed in countries with different data protection laws. When transferring data internationally, we rely on standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions to ensure an equivalent level of protection.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Typically, customer data is retained for 7 years after the last interaction, after which it is anonymized or deleted.

11. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email (if you have an account) or a prominent notice on our Site. The "Last Updated" date at the bottom indicates when the policy was revised. Your continued use of Luxivo after changes signifies acceptance.

12. Contact Our Privacy Team

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out:

  • Privacy Officer Email: privacy@luxivo.com
  • Customer Support: concierge@luxivo.com
  • Mail: Luxivo Privacy Department, 450 Fifth Avenue, Suite 1200, New York, NY 10018, USA

For EU residents, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
